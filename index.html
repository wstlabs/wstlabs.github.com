Currently in Beta

The NYC Landlord Lookup tool, in collaboration with various parties interested in making the NYC real estate data ecosystem more accessible and useful:

    http://lookup.heatseek.org/

It's aggregates some 90M records from 25+ datasets (some well-known, others rather obscure; though not all of them are displayed yet), and is updated monthly. It's hosted in the following 3 repos (at present sparsely documented):

  • REST gateway (Python 3, Flask, PostgreSQL, uWSGI, nginx)
  • single-page frontend (jQuery, Leaflet.js, dust.js)
  • nycprop-identity - a small library of supporting functions
